P – The Basic Paragraph
========================

* Attributes: ALIGN
	* LEFT (default), RIGHT, CENTER. Same as headings.
	* Whitespace ignored (use <BR> for line break)
		- Consecutive <P>’s do not yield multiple blank lines
	* End Tag is Optional:

Example 1:
 <BODY>
 <P>
 Paragraph 1
 </P>
 <P>
 Paragraph 2
 </P>
 <P>
 Paragraph 3
 </P>
 </BODY>
Fully-Specified

Example 2:
 <BODY>
 Paragraph 1
 <P>
 Paragraph 2
 <P>
 Paragraph 3
 </BODY>
Equivalent with Implied Tags
